Summary

The main residence

Building of 170 square metres, with a spectacular view on the nearby coastline, the Monti dell' Uccellina nature reserve, Castiglione della Pescaia and the islands of Elba and Monecristo. It is situated in an area of high scenic value, surrounded by centenary oaks and Mediterranean vegetation.
